Michael DeLally

Web & Game Developer • Multimedia Artist & Writer

GAME DEVELOPER.

Creating virutal worlds is and has been my passion since I was 9 years old. Ever since I could first think of gameplay concepts and stories, I've been trying to turn them into games. Over the course of my adult life I've participated in game jams and am working to publish indie games through Thought Reactor, my multimedia studio.

ASTROPUNK

Currently in development, ASTROPUNK is a story-driven 2.5D rougelite shoot-em-up coming to PC and consoles late 2018. Follow development and watch the game grow!

IGEO

A small puzzle game where you eliminate blocks from the board by moving them together. First commercial release. Originally built in under 24 hours for Ludum Dare 35.

Light Drifter 34

2D driving puzzle game where the player must steer through dangerous courses in an attempt to rack up as many points as possible without dying. Built entirely within 48 hours for Ludum Dare 34.

Pie Quest

Top-down shooting game featuring boss battles and dangerous obstacles over 15 levels. Built entirely within 72 hours for Ludum Dare 37.
